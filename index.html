About Zaid Photography

I’m Zaid, a professional photographer based in Mississauga, Ontario. I specialize in capturing life as it is—raw, real, and unforgettable. From street photography to wedding photography, event coverage, corporate headshots, and real estate photography, I bring a unique perspective to every shot. With my camera in hand, I roam the streets, finding moments that others miss—creating photos that speak and tell your story. No staged poses, no fluff—just genuine moments captured forever. Ready for a photo that stands out? Let’s make it happen.
